Technology

Let's be honest, you want these shoes for the technology. These shoes include a:

    • Wifi Hotspot
      • Keep Wi-fi as you walk.
    • Bluetooth
      • Lets your phone connect to your shoes via bluetooth for other functions.
    • Google Maps Integration
      • If you set your shoes into Sleepwalk Mode,
    • Walk for You
      • With a futuristic combination of gyroscopic technology and vibrations, the shoes will move your legs and feet to walk for you, while keeping your balance.
    • Light Show with Music
      • Lights on the shoes will change in intensity and frequency with the beat and volume of your music, if connected with Bluetooth.
    • Haptic Feedback
      • When not in Sleepwalk Mode, and if you're connected to Bluetooth and set a destination in Google Maps, your shoes will vibrate on the side of the shoe in the direction you need to turn.
